Sparkipedia lets you stay up to date with the newest games, as well as your friends.

By combining an information summary functionality with user profiles and chat options, we can ensure users are always kept in the loop on the newest topics. See our list of future functions below!

  • Timeline comments - See commentary from other users on different events in an IP's timeline.
  • All media type summaries - Sparkipedia will eventually host information on all media entries of a particular IP, not just games.
  • Cross-linked information summaries - See how a character or plot beat intersects and impacts other characters and plot beats with a single click.
  • Plot webs - Individual page summaries a little too oblique? Visualize how different characters and events are related with a plot web.

Let Sparkipedia be your media hub for catching up, keeping up, and sounding off about your favorite games.
